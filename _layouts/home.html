Hi, I'm Kyuha [pronounced as Kyu (as in curious) / ha / Jung (as in jungle)]. I am a Human-Computer Interaction researcher at Seoul National University.



Kyuha Jung is a Master's student in the Department of Communication at Seoul National University. After receiving his Bachelor's degree in Information and Interaction Design at Yonsei University, he continued his academic journey in Communication to incorporate social science perspectives in his user-experience research. Kyuha's research interests focus on designing user-centered solution and social computing tools to tackle user problems about social media and human-AI interaction. He is currently a member of the Human-Computer Interaction+Design (HCI+D) Lab and is being advised by Dr. Joonhwan Lee and Dr. Hajin Lim.
