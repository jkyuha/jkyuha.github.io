Hello! I'm Kyuha.

As of Fall 2022, I’m in my last semester as a Master’s student in Communication at Seoul National University. I am working with Dr. Joonhwan Lee and Dr. Hajin Lim at the Human-Computer Interaction+Design (HCI+D) Lab. In 2020, I received my Bachelor’s degree in Information and Interaction Design at Yonsei University, advised by Dr. Younah Kang. Throughout my undergraduate and Master’s years, I was trained as a qualitative UX researcher with quantitative research skills in social science.

Based on my interdisciplinary background among design and social science, I am interested in improving social media to healthier and more ethical directions. I am often amazed by how people use social media with multi-faceted motivations from which I seek to uncover their needs. I aim to utilize such insights to change the status quo to promote more beneficial social media experiences.

If you have any questions about me or simply want to chat about something, feel free to contact me via email. I love having conversations and exchanging ideas, whether academic or not.
