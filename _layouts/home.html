Hello! I'm Kyuha.

As of Fall 2022, I’m in my last semester as a master’s student in Communication at Seoul National University. I am working with Dr. Joonhwan Lee and Dr. Hajin Lim at the Human-Computer Interaction+Design (HCI+D) Lab. In 2020, I completed my B.S. in Information and Interaction Design at Yonsei University, advised by Dr. Younah Kang. Throughout my undergraduate and master’s years, I was trained as a qualitative UX researcher and designer with quantitative research skills in social science.

Based on my interdisciplinary background among design and social science, I am interested in improving user experience inside social media to healthier and more ethical directions. I aim to utilize user-driven insights to promote better mental health and prevent online risks. Usually, I am curious of people's motivations and behaviors inside social media from which I seek to uncover their needs.

If you have any questions about me or simply want to chat about something, feel free to contact me via email! I love having conversations and exchanging ideas, whether academic or not.
